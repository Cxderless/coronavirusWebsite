Cases

As of the 22nd of August 2020, there are 321,911 coronavirus cases in the United Kingdom. They increase by an average of 90 per day.

Death & recovery figures

As of the 15th of July 2020, there are 62,677 deaths caused by coronavirus. They increase by an average of 20 per day, which has been lowered from an average of 600 per day back in April. The number of people recovered is hidden by the government. For live updates on cases and deaths, see here.
