Who can be tested

The following people can be tested for coronavirus through the NHS.

  • Anyone in England and Wales who has symptoms of coronavirus, no matter their age
  • Anyone in Scotland and Northern Irelia who has symptoms of coronavirus and is older than 5 years old
  • Essential workers in England, Scotland, Wales and Northern Ireland
  • Anyone in England and Wales who has symptoms of coronavirus, who is over the age of 5 and lives with an essential worker
  • Children under 5 years old in England and Wales who have symptoms of coronavirus and live with an essential worker

The previous list is from the UK Government website which also includes guidance on testing for essential workers and the list of essential workers.

What the test involves

If you are an essential worker, you can apply for a coronavirus test through the UK government website here. The test involves taking a swab of the inside of your nose and the back of your throat, using a long cotton bud.
